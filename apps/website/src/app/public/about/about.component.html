About the group

One standard. Many stays.

Teddy City Hotels is a hospitality group that owns, operates, and manages hotels and shortlets — built on the idea that hospitality is a craft.

Our story

Built around the guest.

We started with a single hotel and a clear conviction: the small things — the temperature of the lobby, the quality of breakfast, the response time to a request — are what make a stay memorable. As we've grown, the conviction hasn't changed. Our properties differ in scale and personality, but they all run to one standard.

What we do

Three businesses, one lens.

  • We own hotels.

    Properties under the Teddy City Hotels umbrella, run end-to-end by us.

  • We operate stays.

    Day-to-day hospitality, F&B, and guest experience across the portfolio.

  • We manage for owners.

    The same systems and discipline, applied to other independent hotels.
