About

One standard.
Many stays.

Teddy City Hotels is a small hospitality group from Nigeria. We own, operate and manage hotels — and care, perhaps to a fault, about how a stay feels.

Our story

Built around the guest.

We started with a single hotel and a clear conviction: the small things — the temperature of the lobby, the quality of breakfast, the response time to a request — are what make a stay memorable. As we've grown, the conviction hasn't changed.

Our hotels differ in scale and personality, but they all run to one standard. We choose people who care. We pay attention to what most operators ignore. We keep the front desk close to the guest and the kitchen close to the food.
